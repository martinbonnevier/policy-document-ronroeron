Overview

Ronroeron is a music creation app. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you create — patterns, recordings, samples — stays entirely on your device.

Data Collection

We collect zero data. There are no analytics, no tracking, no telemetry, no crash reporting, and no usage statistics of any kind. No information is ever sent from your device to any server.

Microphone Access

Ronroeron requests access to your device's microphone solely to enable its built-in sampling features:

  • Recording drum samples directly into the drum sequencer
  • Recording sounds into the synth and bass sampler, with automatic pitch detection

All microphone recordings are processed and stored locally on your device only. Audio data is never uploaded, streamed, or transmitted anywhere. The microphone is only active while you are actively recording inside the app.

You can deny microphone permission at any time in your device settings. All other features of the app remain fully functional without it.

Storage

Patterns, settings, and samples you create are saved locally on your device using standard browser storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third Parties

This app contains no third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics services, or data brokers. Your usage is entirely private.

Children's Privacy

Since no data is collected from anyone, this app is safe for all ages under COPPA, GDPR-K, and equivalent regulations worldwide.
